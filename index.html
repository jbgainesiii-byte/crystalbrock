The Story

Beauty runs
in her blood.

Crystal Brock didn't stumble into beauty. She grew up inside it. As a little girl, she played in her mother's makeup. She watched her mother work, push her goals, and earn something remarkable: a brand new Grand AM car that Crystal drove proudly through her entire senior year of high school.

"My mother's dedication to this craft gave me my first car. That's the power of what we do."

Today, Crystal brings that same dedication to the women of Metro Detroit. As a licensed financial literacy educator, credit repair specialist, and Alignable Ambassador, she understands that true confidence goes deeper than makeup.

Questions before you
book?

Do you offer virtual consultations?

Yes. Crystal offers both virtual and in-person consultations for Metro Detroit clients.

What happens during a consultation?

You review skin concerns, makeup preferences, routine, timeline, and budget, then get a personalized recommendation plan.

Do I have to buy products the same day?

No pressure. Crystal walks you through options first, then you decide what fits your needs.

How do I follow up after the chat?

Use the booking button or email directly. Crystal will follow up with next-step recommendations.

How much does a consultation cost?

The initial 30-minute consultation is free. There is no obligation to purchase anything. Crystal will walk you through your options and you decide what fits your needs and budget.

Do you serve clients outside Oak Park?

Yes. Crystal serves all of Metro Detroit virtually and in-person. Virtual sessions are available to anyone in Michigan.
